What We Do

We Groan

Engage at least a million people to pray for missions everyday

We Adopt

Encourage the adoption of at least 3 young missionaries annually

We Give

Execute at least 2 educational project in the 10/40 window annually

OUR VISION

To build a network of young professionals who consistently give to missions projects, go to mission fields and groan for missions work in the 10/40 window.
